Seeking refuge from gangs in Port-au-Prince, Haiti

Over 165,000 people fled their homes in recent years due to gang violence in Haiti. Peeling back the data from an IOM survey in March 2023 gives a look into the where they came from, where they went and how living conditions are now.

By Bennett Hanson
